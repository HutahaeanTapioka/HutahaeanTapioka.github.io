VISION & MISSION
Vision
"To be the best company in Indonesia in producing tapioca flour with environmentally friendly technology"
Mission
  • Participate in building and advancing Tapanuli Raya in the economic field through cassava crop and agriculture program.
  • Improving the welfare of farmers in the economic field by working together for profit sharing in manage their farms.
  • Improving the skills and knowledge of farmers in growing cassava as raw material for producing tapioca flour.
  • Enhance the business experience in an industrial culture in combination with agrarian culture.

PT HUTAHAEAN PABRIK TAPIOKA

PT Hutahaean Tapioka Factory is a company that contributes to the processing of yams in the Toba district.
