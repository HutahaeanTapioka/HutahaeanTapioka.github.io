For workers of a company, most of their time will be spent in the company where they work compared to other places. In addition to giving a sense of boredom, this will certainly reduce time with their families. So that PT HutahaeanPabrikTapioka also conducted Family Gathering activities that were associated as a moment of gathering of workers' families with all company management. Gathering activities can be done such as in tourist sites, recreational parks or in hotels with adequate facilities.
