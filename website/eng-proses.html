Weigh Bridge

Before entering the production stage, first time yams are weighed on the weighbridge to know the net weight

Loading Ramp

As a temporary shelter for cassava before entering the Dry Sieve stage

Dry Sieve

Used as a skin peeler as well as a cleanser for dirt attached to the skin of the sweet potato

Washing

In order to remove the sap/mucus and also cyanide acid (HCN) contained in cassava

Cutting Machine

A tool used to cut cassava into smaller pieces

Rasper

A tool for grinding and grating cassava pieces from the previous cutting process

Extractor

As a tool to separate cassava pulp with water air

Separator

Separator device for hazardous substances to avoid contamination before being discharged into the surrounding environment.

Peeler

Filtration machine for separating different particles/objects, evaporation and cleaning solid-solid mixtures with fine particles with free-flow consistency

Drying Section

Carry out the drying process which will then be sieved

Sifting

The result of drying is in the form of lumps of coarse flour which will be ground and sifted to obtain a finer tapioca flour

Packing

Tapioca flour is ready to be produced, the next stage is the Packing process
