    Company History


    In the process of planting cassava, OPPUNG thought that once the cassava was harvested, where would it be taken? Then without thinking, OPPUNG decided to build a Tapioca Factory to accommodate the marketing of cassava. So in 2009 work began to build a tapioca factory in Pintu Bosi Village while waiting for the cassava to be harvested. View More..

    Owner's Profile

    The owner of PT. Hutahaean Tapioca Factory is Mr. St.Harangan Wilmar Hutahaean who is known by the nickname Mr. Hutahaean or OPPUNG.

    Vision & Mission

    Vision
    "To be the best company in Indonesia in producing tapioca flour with environmentally friendly technology"
    Mission
    1. Participate in building and advancing Tapanuli Raya in the economic field through agriculture and cassava crop programs
    2. Improving the welfare of farmers in the economic field with profit-sharing cooperation in managing their agricultural land
    3. Improve the skills and knowledge of farmers in growing cassava which is the raw material for producing tapioca flour
    4. Improving the experience of doing business in an industrial culture in combination with an agrarian culture
