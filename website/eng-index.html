Company History


In the process of planting these sweet potatoes, OPPUNG thought that if the sweet potatoes had been harvested, where would they be. Then without thinking, OPPUNG decided to build a Tapioca Factory to accommodate the marketing of the sweet potato. So in 2009 work began to build a tapioca factory in Pintubosi Village while waiting for the sweet potatoes to be harvested.

Owner's Profile


The owner of PT. Hutahaean Tapioca Factory is Mr. St. Harangan Wilmar Hutahaean who is known by the nickname Mr. Hutahaean or OPPUNG.

Vision & Mission

Vision
"To be the best company in Indonesia in producing tapioca flour with environmentally friendly technology".
Mission
  1. Participate in building and advancing Tapanuli Raya in the economic field through cassava crop and agriculture program.
  2. Improving the welfare of farmers in the economic field by working together for profit sharing in manage their farms.
  3. Improving the skills and knowledge of farmers in growing cassava as raw material for producing tapioca flour.
  4. Enhance the business experience in an industrial culture in combination with agrarian culture.
