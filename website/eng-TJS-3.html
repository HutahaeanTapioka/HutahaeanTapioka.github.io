Right Program

Education

PT Hutahaean Tapioka Factory also not infrequently pays special attention to the education around the company.

By providing assistance both in the form of building repairs and other facilities such as cleaning tools, flower pots and so on.

It is also a form of corporate responsibility by having a positive impact on the world of education around the company.

Community

To get input from the community, PT Hutahaean Tapioka Factory once a year conducts meetings with community leaders or envoys.

Inputs provided include waste management, CSR programs, acceptance of sweet potatoes, competing dreg prices, and also proposals both for spiritual activities and for the improvement of the populist economy.

The meeting went well and the company got new ideas as well as new creations.
