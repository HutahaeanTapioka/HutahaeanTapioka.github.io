TTKKYY

Production Code

Packaging of tapioca flour Cap Beringin production pt. Hutahaean has a production code to mark the product so that it is not incorporated in the product with other factories that have been packaged in the same production time.

TT

Indicates the location/place of the factory producing tapioca flour

KK

Indicates how many kilograms of tapioca flour (25 &50) kg

YY

Indicates production date in sync with expiration date (0-9)

Composition

1. Made from 100% Cassava with varieties such as UJ-5; Adira-4; Malasya; and others.
2. Good for food raw materials, cakes, noodles, empeks and other types of snacks.
3. For adhesive materials for carton mills and food wrapping paper as well as bleaching materials for paper mills
4. For companies that use environmentally friendly technology tapioca flour can be used as a raw material helpers to make organic bags that are environmentally friendly.
5. And other necessities.

Standard expiration period of tapioca flour with a period of 6 (six) months from the date of production.

Lizard Time

DD

0 – 9 can be changed for needs indicates expiration Day

MM

0 – 9 can be changed for needs indicates the expiration of the Month

YYY

0 – 9 can be changed for needs indicates the expiration of the Year
